Concept Iteration
When the gülsha team approached us for our design expertise, they had a clear vision in mind: to design a unified experience that meets people’s needs while ensuring consistency within the brand. We spent weeks with past, existing, and future retail journeys getting to know their unique perspectives and needs. This way, our conceptual design and digital branding reflected the empathy and nuance required to adequately support buyers’ needs while effectively delivering the correct way to use the gülsha products.
