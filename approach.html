Our human-centred design approach allows us to understand our customers’ needs, identify with their stance and create solutions accordingly in order to enhance the ultimate experience of the brand and the product.

Design Thinking is integral to how we translate ideas and strategy into a real used product.

The key factor of our success is the fact the user is in the center of our design throughout the entire process.

Our design process is iterative; therefore the stages can be modified, interchanged and adapted based on the project or the product to help continuously improve design.

Research

This is the step where we develop a deep user understanding and contextualize your product. Users’ motivations, actions, expectations, and rationales are learned by studying your company, your users, your market space and your industry.

Analysis & Evaluation

We combine what is gathered while observing and engaging with users directly in the relevant environment and define the problems we are facing with. Using these problems as focus points based on brainstorming sessions we come up with all possible strategies and start working on the information architecture, wireframes and user flows.

Test

Whether it’s websites, apps, or custom software, Freyjan’s UX & UI design team masterfully crafts for the end-user, flawlessly operates in an Agile environment, and continually conduct usability tests, using rapid and interactive prototyping. This way, our design team identifies friction in the user experience early on, and repair it before deploying.

Design

We design user interfaces that increase conversion, reduce cost of maintenance and build a consistent user experience. Our user-friendly interfaces reflect your brand strategy, visual identity, and verbal articulation. Ultimately our designs establish a prominent position within the market while achieving quantifiable business goals.
