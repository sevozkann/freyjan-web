Concept Designs

For the new betting project we worked on three different concepts. These conceptual iterations bring forth a combination of the UX findings and the user behaviour analysis gathered after studies conducted throughout one-on-one meetings.

The second concept design was based off of clear white space and the use of light colours as it conveyed legibility. The main goal was to promote the sports world rather than that of gambling. The outcome addressed an established brand maintaining power, clarity and precision.

The most prominence of the first concept design was the middle ground between the notion of “risk/challenge” and “fun/transparency” — we sought for an experience that delivered a combination of the both. Designing a betting app requires a thorough research of sports statistics, which inadvertently creates a serious connotation. Regardless, we wanted the users to feel comfortable and in control during the process.
